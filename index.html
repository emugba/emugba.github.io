Welcome to EmuGBA repository page!

Click here to download EmuGBA for iOS devices. Create a Google Account. You will need it when adding a ROM. Created by Tomo Yamamoto for a computer science project on HTML5 emulator.

Features

Version 1.5

  • Remembers your ROM save state allowing you to continue playing where you left off.
  • 8-bit audio.
  • Store all your Game Boy and Game Boy Color ROM and cover art in Google Drive.
